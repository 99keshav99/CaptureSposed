# Privacy Policy for CaptureSposed

**Effective Date:** June 3, 2026

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy applies to the **CaptureSposed** Android application (the "App"), developed as an open-source project. CaptureSposed is an Xposed module designed to disable screenshot and screen recording detection APIs on Android devices. 

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices. 

## 2. Data Collection and Use
**CaptureSposed does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data.**

The App operates entirely locally on your device. We do not use analytics, tracking frameworks, background telemetry, or data collection software development kits (SDKs). No information regarding your device hardware, your usage of the App, the apps you use, or your personal identity is ever transmitted to the developer or any third parties.

## 3. Required Permissions and Device Access
To function correctly, CaptureSposed requires root access and the LSPosed framework. These elevated privileges are used strictly locally to hook into system APIs and prevent applications from detecting when you take a screenshot or record your screen. 

* **Local Processing Only:** All system modifications and module operations are performed locally on your device.
* **No Remote Access:** The developer has no access to your device, the screenshots you take, or the screen recordings you capture.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The App does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics providers. Because no data is collected, no user data is ever shared with, sold to, or accessible by third parties.

## 5. Open Source Transparency
CaptureSposed is open-source software licensed under the AGPL-3.0 license. The complete source code is publicly available on GitHub. Anyone can independently review and audit the code to verify that no undocumented data collection or transmission occurs.

## 6. Children's Privacy
CaptureSposed does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. Given the technical nature of the App — which requires a rooted device and the LSPosed framework — it is not directed at or intended for use by children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so it can be deleted.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since the App collects no data, any changes will likely reflect updates to App functionality or new Google Play regulatory requirements. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy and publishing the revised policy on our GitHub repository.
